                                           ** E - Commerce Sales Analysis **

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes an e - commerce superstore sales dataset that covers business insights related to sales performance, profits and lossses, sales per region and also recommends actionable strategies. The tools used are SQL and Tableau for visualization and insights.

**Objectives**

*Analyze overall sales and profits

*Top and least performing categories

*Regional sales distribution

*Improving areas where sales and profit performance is less

**Tools & Technologies**

* SQL (SQLite) - Data analysis and querying
* Tableau - Data visualization and dashboard creation
* Github - Project documentation

**Dashboard**

The dashboard gives insights on
* Sales per category
* Sales per Region
* Profits by category
* Profit and sales analysis
* Top and Least category analysis

**Key Insights**

* Sales and Profit margins are heavily concentrated in Technology category, making it a key focus area for growth.
* In contast, certain sub - categories related to Furniture and office supplies sales decreased due to aggressive discounts.
* Regional performance is better in some areas  in West and East side, while most of the areas on the other sides and more contributing to revenue than profits.
* The analysis indicates that higher sale sdo not always gives higher profits, but also depends upon balanced and market specific strategies.

**Business Recommendations**

* Limiting excessive discounts and focusing on profit margins on products which have low margin
* Focusing on high performing categories like Technology to drive growth
* Implementing pricing strategy rather than excessive discounts for underperforming categories.
* Focusing on market specific strategies that are least performing based on that region

**Conclusion**

This project demonstrates how the data analysis that helps to take better business decisions by identifying profits and sales.
